# Guild Ability Prompt Review

## Purpose

This file is the pre-production alignment sheet for guild ability visuals.

The workflow should be:

1. Read and tweak the plain-language ability descriptions here.
2. Agree on the gameplay read and fantasy.
3. Decide whether the effect belongs in actor-attached rendering, projectile rendering, impact VFX, or some mix.
4. Only then generate or wire the visuals.

This is intentionally not a rendering plan yet.

## Adventurer

- `Rallying Cry` — a short self-and-allies battle shout that makes the party move and hit harder for a few seconds.
- `Slash` — a straightforward close-range sword slash in a short forward cone.
- `Bandage` — a brief self-heal channel where the adventurer patches themselves up under pressure.
- `Quickshot` — a fast, practical ranged shot fired straight ahead.
- `Adrenaline Rush` — a full combat overdrive where the adventurer speeds up and hits harder for a short burst.
- `Second Wind` — a quick reset that restores stamina and gets the adventurer back into the fight.

## Knight

- `Holy Rebuke` — a point-blank holy shockwave that stuns enemies around the knight.
- `Valorous Strike` — a firm melee hit that builds the knight’s resolve.
- `Taunt` — a commanding shout that forces enemies nearby to focus the knight.
- `Shield Wall` — a protective defensive stance that reduces incoming damage for allies near the knight.
- `Last Stand` — a heroic survival state where the knight refuses to fall and becomes much more dangerous for a short window.
- `Shield Block` — a tight defensive utility move that heavily reduces damage for a moment.

## Mage

- `Ice Nova` — a freezing burst centered on the mage that roots enemies caught in it.
- `Frostbolt` — a cold projectile fired forward that slows whatever it hits.
- `Blink` — a fast magical reposition that instantly moves the mage a long distance.
- `Arcane Shard` — a piercing magical projectile that punches through targets in a line.
- `Meteor` — a delayed ground-targeted magical nuke that slams down in a large area.
- `Short Teleport` — a smaller, faster version of blink used as a quick reposition.

## Druid

- `Wild Growth` — a healing nature circle around the druid that restores health and leaves regen on allies.
- `Entangle` — a nature projectile that roots the enemy it catches.
- `Rejuvenate` — a direct heal on a nearby ally followed by a heal-over-time effect.
- `Cleanse` — a supportive heal that also strips debuffs off an ally.
- `Tranquility` — a long healing channel where the druid steadily restores allies in a large area.
- `Shapeshift` — a transformation toggle that swaps the druid into animal forms.

## Hunter

- `Disengage` — a backward escape move with a brief blinding effect around the hunter.
- `Piercing Volley` — multiple arrows fired in a line that can pass through enemies.
- `Aimed Shot` — a deliberate charged arrow with stronger single-target payoff.
- `Bear Trap` — a placed ground trap that roots enemies when triggered.
- `Rain of Arrows` — a channel that saturates a targeted area with arrows and slows enemies inside.
- `Pet Command` — a utility command that redirects the hunter’s pet or changes its behavior.

## Monk

- `Serenity` — a brief untouchable state that cleanses crowd control and boosts speed.
- `Flying Kick` — a fast lunging kick that carries the monk forward and knocks enemies down.
- `Jab` — a quick basic strike that builds chi.
- `Five-Point Palm` — a precise melee strike that plants a delayed detonation on the target.
- `Dragon's Fury` — a rapid strike barrage channel that ends with a heavy finishing stun.
- `Parry` — a tight defensive timing window that punishes attackers if read correctly.

## Viking

- `Whirlwind` — the viking spins through nearby enemies in a furious melee channel, sustaining through lifesteal while rotating in place.
- `Harpoon` — the viking throws a literal harpoon forward and yanks the target back toward them.
- `Bloodlust` — the viking enters a red-hot berserker frenzy with faster swings and lifesteal for several seconds.
- `Axe Swing` — a heavy broad axe sweep that hits close enemies with raw strength and builds rage.
- `Undying Rage` — the viking swells into a near-unkillable berserk state, refusing death for a short duration before the effect ends in a heal.
- `Shield Bash` — a blunt close-range bash that smashes the enemy backward.

## Prophet

- `Prophetic Shield` — a holy protective barrier that absorbs damage and converts the broken shield into healing.
- `Smite` — a holy projectile fired at range that marks the target with revelation.
- `Bless` — a direct ally buff that increases damage and movement for a while.
- `Curse` — a hostile mark placed on an enemy that makes them weaker and more vulnerable.
- `Divine Intervention` — a miracle-grade protection effect that makes an ally untouchable before restoring them fully.
- `Divine Insight` — a revealing pulse that exposes all nearby enemies.

## Vampire

- `Hemorrhage` — a ranged blood attack that causes bleeding damage over time and feeds overflow back to the vampire.
- `Shadow Step` — a sudden teleport behind an enemy followed by a short stealth window.
- `Blood Drain` — a channel that siphons life directly from the target into the vampire.
- `Fang Strike` — a vicious close-range bite or slash that heals through lifesteal.
- `Nocturne` — a full predator state with stealth and speed, empowering the next strike from the shadows.
- `Mist Step` — a smaller evasive teleport wrapped in brief stealth.

## Cultist

- `Summon Spawn` — a ritual cast that tears open reality long enough to call in an allied horror.
- `Whispers` — a psychic projectile that damages and briefly silences the victim.
- `Madness` — a wide psychic affliction that confuses, stuns, and damages enemies around the target area.
- `Tendril Grasp` — a ground eruption of grasping necrotic tendrils that roots and damages enemies.
- `Open the Gate` — a dangerous channel that opens into a huge psychic catastrophe, pulling enemies inward and blasting them apart.
- `Gaze into Abyss` — a self-buff that makes the next cultist cast cheaper and stronger.

## Champion

- `Tithe of Blood` — the champion spends built-up blood power to heal and become much faster in combat.
- `Berserker Charge` — a brutal forward rush that bowls through enemies and builds more blood power.
- `Execute` — a heavy melee finisher that becomes especially deadly against weakened enemies.
- `Cleaver` — a broad chopping strike that hits enemies in a short melee arc.
- `Skullsplitter` — a devastating ultimate melee blow built to finish targets with overwhelming force.
- `Challenge` — a hostile mark that dares an enemy to face the champion and take extra punishment from them.

## Darkmage

- `Darkness` — a placed shadow zone that blinds and obscures enemies caught inside it.
- `Grasping Shadow` — a shadow projectile that roots on impact.
- `Soul Leech` — a ranged siphon that steals life or essence from the victim.
- `Shadow Bolt` — a damaging dark projectile that stacks chilling slowdown.
- `Eternal Night` — a large cursed zone that silences and rots enemies over time.
- `Shadow Cloak` — a stealthy movement state that hides the darkmage briefly.

## Chef

- `Feast` — a wide supportive cast that serves a dish buff to allies in range.
- `Ladle Bash` — a comedic but forceful melee smack that briefly dazes the target.
- `Hot Soup` — a healing toss that restores health and leaves a warm regeneration effect.
- `Spice Toss` — a thrown seasoning attack that blinds and irritates enemies over time.
- `Signature Dish` — a theatrical cooking channel that empowers all allies with a stacked meal effect.
- `Pocket Dish` — a self-use item action where the chef quickly consumes a prepared buff.

## Leper

- `Plague Vomit` — a diseased cone burst that infects and slows enemies in front of the leper.
- `Diseased Claw` — a close infected swipe that leaves rotting damage over time.
- `Necrotic Embrace` — a brutal grab-and-drain strike that heals the leper from the damage dealt.
- `Contagion` — an infection mark placed on a target that can spread through the battlefield.
- `Rotting Tide` — a necrotic channel that erupts outward and can raise fresh husks from the dead.
- `Miasma` — a toggled toxic aura that constantly damages enemies who stay too close.

## Master

- `Chosen Strike` — a flexible class-themed strike whose expression depends on the master’s current primed class.
- `Chosen Utility` — a class-themed mobility or utility action drawn from the current primed style.
- `Chosen Nuke` — a stronger burst attack flavored by the currently primed class.
- `Eclipse` — a state where the master rapidly cycles through class aspects for a short time.
- `Apotheosis` — a peak mastery state where cooldowns drop and the master becomes stronger and self-sustaining.
- `Class Swap` — the master rotates to the next primed class identity.
