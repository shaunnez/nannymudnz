## Key facts

- Founded: April 20, 1990 by Mats Persson
- Host: Lysator, Linköping University, Sweden
- Codebase: NannyMOS (LPMud 3.1.2 fork); mudlib originally based on LPMud 2.4.5
- Theme: Epic medieval fantasy with magic; heavy pastiche of Tolkien, Dragonlance, Warhammer, Lovecraft, Wheel of Time, Arthurian legend
- Scale: 200+ areas, 50,000+ rooms, 136 quests, 15 guilds, many clubs
- High wizards (historical): Beldin, Titleist, Vulcan, Astrodeath
- Local god: Mats ("beware of his wrath")
- Pillars of play: PK, hack-and-slash, questing, social chatting
